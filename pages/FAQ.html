If you don't have any goals defined, you will be taken to your playlist. If you want to set goals, click here!

You will only see the custom feed if you have defined goals. If you don't have any goals set, you won't see it.

You have the option to choose:

  • When you want to watch the video feed of your favourite subscribed YouTubers. Click the extension and toggle it on or off if you want to see subs. Of course, you won't see any videos if you haven't subscribed to anyone yet.
  • Custom feed based on your goals: You can enter, if you click on the extension and click on the button "Define your goals" you can enter your goals and after that you can enter youtube normally and the custom feed with videos based on your goals will be displayed.

We recommend that you are logged in within YouTube. Otherwise a lot of things won't work.

If you've found a bug or error in the extension, you can go here and submit your request. We will get back to you shortly.
